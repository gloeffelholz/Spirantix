First to Launch

Emery

Memory care, powered by your own life

AI-powered memory support for people living with dementia and Alzheimer's. Emery helps users remember the faces, events, and places of their own lives. The name is its own mnemonic: one sound away from "memory," easy to call out when you need it.

Take or upload a photo and simply ask: "Who is this?" Emery recognizes family and friends from a private knowledge base built from the user's own photo library, and answers warmly and conversationally. Ask about past events, browse pictures of loved ones, or get gentle reminders about what's coming up.

Family members or caregivers set it up once with the photos and names that matter; from then on it's as simple as asking. All faces and personal information stay on the family's own infrastructure. Nothing is shared, sold, or used for training.

"Who is this?" "Tell me about our trip to the Cape" "Show me pictures of Sophie" "What's happening tomorrow?"

Addie

Your photo albums, your voice, your legacy

Remember building photo albums by hand: choosing the pictures, putting them in order, telling the story behind each page? Addie brings that to your digital photos. Built especially for older adults who want their pictures to mean something to the people who come after them.

Create albums from the events Capsa organizes, then add the part no photo can capture: record yourself telling the story. The Slideshow feature plays your photos with your own voice narrating, in your words, for your grandchildren and theirs.

Every album is sealed, so future generations can trust these photos and stories came from you, exactly as you left them.

Capsa

Every photo you own, organized in one place you control

Your photos are scattered across Google Photos, iCloud, Dropbox, old laptops, and external drives. Capsa, named for the case Romans used to protect the scrolls they cared enough to keep, consolidates all of them into a clean Year, Event, and Date hierarchy on hardware you own.

The AI sees only small thumbnails, just enough to sort intelligently. Your full-resolution photos never leave your machine. Confident matches are organized automatically; anything uncertain is set aside for your review. The events and groups Capsa creates become the raw material for Addie albums and Emery's memory support.

S

Sealed with PhotoSeal

PhotoSeal is the trust layer behind Spirantix products: a cryptographic sealing protocol that certifies a file was authenticated by its owner at a specific moment in time, and shows whether it has been modified since. Built as an open, documented standard, so your files can be verified by anyone, with any tool, for decades to come.
