Emery

AI memory support that feels like family.

Emery is an AI-powered memory support companion for people living with dementia and Alzheimer's. She recognizes the faces, events, and places that matter most, using a private knowledge base built from the user's own photos and information. Emery is here to help spark joy, reduce confusion, and bring comfort through familiar memories and gentle conversation.

Join the Emery Beta

Mission

To help people feel connected, confident, and seen by remembering what matters most to them.

What Emery does

Remembers

Recognizes people, places, and moments that are meaningful to you.

"Who is this?"

Engages

Answers questions about past events and what's coming up.

"What's happening tomorrow?"

Shares

Lets you browse photos of loved ones and special memories.

"Show me pictures of Sophie."

Supports

Provides gentle reminders and reassurance, every day.

"Tell me about our trip to the Cape."

How Emery works

1

Add photos & names

Family or caregivers upload photos and personal information, once.

2

Private knowledge base

Emery learns privately from the user's own library. Nothing is shared or sold.

3

Ask anything

Users simply ask questions in natural language, the way they'd ask family.

4

Helpful, warm responses

Emery responds with personalized, comforting answers.
