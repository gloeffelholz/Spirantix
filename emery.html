Emery

AI memory support that feels like family.

For someone living with dementia, Alzheimer's, or short-term memory loss, the real challenge is getting through a normal day. Emery is a memory support companion built for exactly that. She keeps each day on track by managing your calendar and task list, building a simple daily plan, and offering gentle reminders. And she remembers the faces, events, and places that matter most, using a private knowledge base built from the user's own photos and information. Emery is here to help spark joy, reduce confusion, and bring comfort through familiar memories and gentle conversation.

How Emery works

1

Add photos & names

Family or caregivers upload photos and personal information, once.

2

Private knowledge base

Emery learns privately from the user's own library. Nothing is shared or sold.

3

Ask anything

Users simply ask questions in natural language, the way they'd ask family.

4

Helpful, warm responses

Emery responds with personalized, comforting answers.
