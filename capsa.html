Capsa

Your photos, your albums, your stories. In one place you control.

Capsa brings all your photos and memories together, no matter where they're stored, and helps you turn them into beautiful albums and storytelling experiences. From scattered snapshots to narrated slideshows, Capsa organizes, enhances, and preserves your legacy exactly the way you want it.

Mission

To help you gather, organize, and share your memories through albums and stories that last for generations.

What Capsa does

Gathers

Finds photos across all your devices and cloud accounts and brings them together.

"All your photos. All in one place."

Organizes

Sorts and organizes by people, dates, places, and events, so you can find anything.

"Finally, a home for every memory."

Narrates

Creates slideshows with your photos and your voice. Your stories, in your words.

"This is our story. Told your way."

Protects

Your full-resolution photos stay on your device or local storage, with optional cloud backup.

"Private by design. Yours to control."
